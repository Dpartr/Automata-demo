Conway's Game of Life

This is a Demo of Conway's game of life. The rules are.

  1. A live Cell will die if it has less than 2 Neighbors
  2. A live cell will die if it has more than 3 Neighbors
  3. A Dead cell will be born if it has exactly 3 Neighbors

The Game of Life has several Organisms. Use their cooresponding button to place it on the grid and see it evolve. For example, placing the Blossom on a blank sheet will create a beautiful symmetric animation. Press Generate Noise to Randomly toggle cells to alive.

If you are viewing this on a desktop browser, hover your mouse over the grid to interactively change cell states.
